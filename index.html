About Me


Welcome to my about me page!














My name is Luke Evans. I was born and raised in Gold Hill, North Carolina on a Tenessee walking horse farm.
After leaving college I wanted to get my feet wet in multiple industrys to figure out what I liked.
I've worked construction, the restauraunt industry, car sales, and event setup/management.
These jobs fortunately taught me what I did not want to do with the rest of my life.

I made the leap and applied to one of my favorite video game companies (EpicGames) for an entry level QA position working on Fortnite Save the world.

Soon after I moved to the Performance/Memory team as a software tester to help develop and optomize Fortnite Battle Royale, and Creative mode.

I helped bring Fortnite to Switch, Android, and iOS.

I also helped with crossplatform efforts to bridge the gap between platforms helping connect millions of people.

For the past three years I have been working diligently to help make Fortnite one of the best video games in the world!
