About Me

Hello and welcome to my about me page!

My name is Luke Evans. I was born and raised in Gold Hill North Carolina on a Tenessee walking horse farm. After leaving college I started within construction, the restauraunt industry, car sales, and event setup/management. These jobs taught me what I did not want to do with the rest of my life.

From working in these industries my obsession with videogames quickly became a burning desire that I wanted to pursue. I made the leap and applied to one of my favorite video game companies (EpicGames) for an entry level QA position working on Fortnite Save the world. Soon after I transitioned to the Performance and Memory team as a software tester to help develop and optomize Fortnite Battle Royale and Creative mode. I worked diligently for the past 2 years to help make Fortnite one of the best video games in the world.
